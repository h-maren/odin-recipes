Yummy Effin Homemade Garlic Bread

Homemade garlic bread looking delicious af

Why should you use perfectly good easy to cook frozen garlic bread when you can slave away to make something slightly better? Cause principal, that's why.

Ingredients

  • Lb italian or french loaf - LB god damn! that's a lot of bread
  • 1/2 cup softened unsalted butter, or salted if you want to die sooner
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced - or you can be lazy and get the kind in a jar
  • Tb fresh parsley - or not fresh you lazy piece of shit
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt - can you even find 1/8 tsp?!?!?!
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan - store bought if you suck

Recipe Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. Slice bread in half lengthwise and place on baking sheet, cut sides up.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix butter, garlic, parsley and salt together until well combined. Spread evenly over bread.
  4. Bake for 10-15 mins or until slightly golden brown on the edges. If you want to add cheese (why wouldn't you????), add it the last 2 mins of baking.
