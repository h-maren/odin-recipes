Haley's Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Mmmm don't this lasagna look good as hell.

Usually people write a bunch of stuff about how their 80 year old grandma made this for their italian boyfriend or something but I don't feel like writing all that.

Ingredients

  • Lb Ground meat
  • 4 cups tomato sauce - homemade or not
  • Garlic - a lot unless you're a puss
  • Onion - or not, who cares
  • Box Lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz Ricotta
  • 1 Egg
  • Parsley - who cares if it's fresh or not
  • Shit ton of parmesan and mozarella
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe Steps

  1. Make the tomato sauce if not homemade.
  2. Boil the noodles at some point.
  3. Mix tomato sauce, garlic and ground beef.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg and parsley.
  5. Preheat oven to 325 F.
  6. To assemble, put cup of meat sauce in lasagna pan. Layer with noodles, then spread half the riccota mixture over it and 1/3 of mozarella. Spoon another cup of meat sauce, sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  7. Do another layer, then cover with rest of mozarella and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil, not letting it touch. Bake 25 mins.
  9. Remove foil and bake another 25 mins.
  10. Let it rest at least 15 mins cause it'll be hot af.
